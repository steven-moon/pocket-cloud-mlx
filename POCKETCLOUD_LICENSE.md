PocketCloud follows an **open-source-first, sustainable** licensing model designed to:

- **Free Access**: Enable everyone to run private, local AI on their own hardware  
- **Community Growth**: Foster open collaboration and innovation
- **Project Sustainability**: Generate revenue from commercial success while keeping the core accessible
- **Privacy by Design**: Promote local-first, private AI that users fully control

### What is Always Free

You may use PocketCloud at no charge if **any** of the following is true:

1. **Personal Use**: You are an individual using it for yourself, including at work
2. **Small Organizations**: Your company has < 10 employees AND < $1M USD in annual revenue  
3. **Early Products**: The product or service that uses PocketCloud generates < $250k USD in annual revenue

If any of these apply, PocketCloud is **free with no registration required**.

### Commercial Licensing

If your organization or product **exceeds these thresholds** AND PocketCloud is a **core component** of:
- A product or service you sell
- Internal tools that drive significant cost savings or revenue

You must obtain a **commercial license** from the Pocket Cloud Foundation.

[*Contact us to discuss commercial licensing*](mailto:foundation@pocketcloud.org)

## ðŸ¤ Contributor License Agreement (CLA)

All contributions to PocketCloud projects require signing a Contributor License Agreement once per identity. This ensures the Foundation has clear rights to distribute your code under both community and commercial licenses.

### What the CLA Does

- **Grants Rights**: Provides the Pocket Cloud Foundation a broad, irrevocable license to use, modify, distribute, and create derivatives of your contributions
- **Preserves Your Rights**: You maintain copyright ownership of your code  
- **Enables Licensing**: Allows the Foundation to offer both community and commercial licenses
- **Patent Protection**: Includes patent license for contributions

### CLA Signing Process

1. **First Contribution**: Submit a Pull Request to any PocketCloud repository
2. **CLA Check**: A bot automatically checks if you've signed the CLA
3. **Sign Online**: Follow the provided link to sign electronically via GitHub OAuth
4. **One-Time Only**: Future contributions from your account are automatically approved
